Who We Are

Define. Deliver. Prove.

Raise Fwd is a consulting firm for nonprofits, mission-driven organizations, and corporate CSR verticals ready to operate at their full potential.

We build the frameworks and optimize operations. You own the results. Every engagement leaves your organization more capable, not more dependent — with the systems and strategies to turn mission into measurable impact.

  • Deep sector expertise across strategy, governance, executive leadership, and fundraising
  • Practical frameworks built for accountability and real momentum
  • A philanthropic lens — we understand donor psychology and what moves people to give
  • Flexible models — project, fractional, or interim — sized to your needs and budget
What We Do

Consulting Services

We offer a focused range of services designed to strengthen the core of your nonprofit — from strategy to sustainability.

Strategic Planning

Facilitate board and staff alignment around a clear theory of change and actionable multi-year strategy. We guide your organization through environmental scanning, priority-setting, and the development of a roadmap that connects mission to measurable outcomes.

Planning & Vision Communications Plan

Fundraising Operations & Tech Stack

Build the infrastructure your development vertical needs to sustain and grow — and make sure your technology supports it. We assess systems, processes, and team capacity, and identify the most efficient and cost-effective CRM and tech solutions to optimize how your organization attracts, cultivates, and retains donors at every level.

Revenue & Sustainability Technology & Systems

Development Planning

Create a comprehensive, multi-channel development plan that diversifies your revenue base. From annual funds and major gifts to grants and planned giving, we design strategies that are ambitious, realistic, and rooted in your unique donor community.

Growth Strategy

Development Coaching

Provide personalized coaching for development and leadership professionals seeking to sharpen their skills and advance their impact. We offer one-on-one guidance, goal-setting, and real-time support to help fundraisers and leaders navigate challenges and grow with confidence.

Coaching & Growth

Board Development

Strengthen governance, deepen board engagement, and align your board’s capacity with your organization’s strategic direction. We work with executive directors and board chairs to build cultures of accountability, effective meeting structures, and boards that lead with confidence.

Access seasoned C-suite leadership exactly when you need it. We provide fractional CEO, COO, and Chief Fundraising Officer support on an ongoing basis, as well as interim executive leadership during transitions and vacancies — giving your organization senior capacity without the full-time cost.
